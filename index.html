THE
ANIME
GAME GUY.

I'm Globku — a content creator and game critic who has spent over a decade focused specifically on anime games. Not as a side category, but as a genuine specialization.

Anime games occupy a unique space in the industry. They're often made for dedicated fanbases, developed on varied budgets, and evaluated by audiences who bring deep investment to every release. Generic criticism rarely captures what makes them succeed or fail on their own terms.

This site exists to bring informed, honest written critique to that space — the kind that takes the genre seriously without excusing its flaws, and recognizes its strengths without overhyping them.

Scores are given out of 100. They reflect the complete experience: gameplay depth, presentation, value, and how well the game delivers on its specific promises.

HOW I REVIEW

01

Full Playthrough

Reviews are based on complete playthroughs, not preview builds. I don't publish until I've seen the credits and spent meaningful time with endgame content where applicable.

02

Genre Context

Anime games are reviewed within their genre and alongside comparable titles — not against AAA blockbusters. A solid arena fighter is evaluated as an arena fighter, not as if it's competing with Elden Ring.

03

IP Awareness

I understand the source material. Whether it's Dragon Ball, Naruto, or a niche gacha universe, I review knowing what the fanbase expects and whether the game delivers on those specific promises.
